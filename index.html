South Tyrol's Shuttle App

Shuttle Finder is an app that helps users to find a hire car with driver. The user selects his district or uses GPS and calls a cab directly via the app.

How it works

Step 1

Select your district or use GPS

Step 2

Select your Shuttle

Step 3

Call directly via the app

Ratings

Ratings enhance transparency and security. Users can rate Shuttle services by the following criteria:

  • Service
  • Reliability & Punctuality
  • Driving Style & Security
  • Price
